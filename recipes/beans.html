Beans

Simple baked beans

This simple recipe takes some canned baked beans with pork and adds a touch of extra flavour using bacon, onion and some other ingredients easily found in your store cupboard.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 (16oz) cans of baked beans and pork
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 4 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 2 slices of bacon, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175C. Grease a casserole dish with cooking spray.
  2. Mix together all other ingredients except the bacon in a large bowl. Transfer the mixture to the casserole dish and add the chopped bacon to the top.
  3. Cover and bake in the oven until thickened, about 3 hours.

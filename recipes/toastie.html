Pesto grilled cheese sandwich

An adventurous grilled cheese sandwich using the best kind of cheeses for melting and adding the complementary flavour of red onion along with the delicious contrast of pesto.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon prepared pesto sauce
  • 1 slice provolone cheese
  • 1 slice american cheese
  • some thinly sliced red onion

Steps

  1. place sliced cheese on one slice of bread
  2. add the red onion on top
  3. add the pesto to the top
  4. place another slice of bread on top
  5. place in a sandwich grill and cook until the cheese has melted
